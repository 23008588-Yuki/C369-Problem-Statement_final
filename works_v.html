Some of the works we have done:

Financial Literacy Workshops

Through the use of FInancial Literacy Workshops, we brough together many youths from that were interested to pick up financial literacy, and engaged them through a series of workshops carried out online.

Interactive Webinars

Our series of interactive webinars on financial literacy is designed to equip youth with essential skills and knowledge to manage their finances effectively and confidently. Through engaging and practical sessions, participants will gain insights into various aspects of personal finance, empowering them to make informed financial decisions from an early age.

Money Management Challenges

Our Money Management Challenge is a dynamic program designed to immerse youth in practical financial scenarios, fostering hands-on learning and skill development in money management. Participants engage in a series of interactive challenges and activities aimed at equipping them with essential financial skills necessary for navigating real-world financial decisions.
