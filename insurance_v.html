Health Insurance (Medishield Life / Integrated Shield Plans)

Coverage: Provides coverage for hospitalization, surgery, and certain outpatient treatments. Medishield Life is a basic health insurance plan provided by the government, while Integrated Shield Plans (offered by private insurers) enhance coverage beyond what Medishield Life provides.

Benefits: Covers medical expenses, hospital stays, surgical procedures, and some outpatient treatments. Integrated Shield Plans may offer additional benefits such as coverage for private hospital stays and higher ward class options.

Average cost: Medishield Life premiums are subsidized and vary based on age and income. Integrated Shield Plans (private insurers) cost between SGD 300 to SGD 2,000 per year, depending on coverage and age.

Life Insurance

Coverage: Provides financial protection to beneficiaries in case of the policyholder's death. It may also offer benefits for critical illness or total permanent disability (optional riders).

Benefits: Pays out a lump sum or regular payments to beneficiaries upon the insured's death, ensuring financial security for dependents. Some policies may accumulate cash value over time.

Average cost: Term life insurance premiums can range from SGD 200 to SGD 1,000 per year, depending on coverage amount and age. Whole life or investment-linked policies may cost more, with premiums varying significantly based on coverage and features.

Personal Accident Insurance

Coverage: Provides compensation for injuries, disability, or death caused by accidents. Coverage may include medical expenses, disability income, and death benefits.

Benefits: Offers financial support for medical treatments, income replacement during recovery, and lump-sum payments for permanent disability or death resulting from accidents.

Average cost: Personal accident insurance premiums typically range from SGD 100 to SGD 500 per year, depending on coverage limits and benefits.

Travel Insurance

Coverage: Covers travel-related risks such as trip cancellations, lost baggage, medical emergencies abroad, and personal liability.

Benefits: Reimburses non-refundable travel expenses due to cancellations, provides emergency medical assistance and evacuation, compensates for lost or delayed baggage, and covers personal liability in case of accidents or damages caused to others during travel.

Average cost: Single trip travel insurance may cost between SGD 20 to SGD 100 for a short trip, while annual travel insurance plans range from SGD 200 to SGD 500, depending on coverage and duration of trips.

Home Insurance (Fire Insurance / Home Contents Insurance)

Coverage: Protects against losses or damages to the structure of the home (fire insurance) and contents inside the home (home contents insurance).

Benefits: Covers repair or replacement costs due to fire, theft, natural disasters, and other specified risks. Home contents insurance may also cover personal belongings, appliances, and valuable items against damage or loss.

Average cost: Fire insurance premiums for HDB flats start from around SGD 1.50 per month. Home contents insurance for a typical HDB flat can cost around SGD 80 to SGD 150 per year, depending on coverage and location.

Motor Insurance (Car Insurance / Motorcycle Insurance)

Coverage: Mandatory third-party liability coverage for injuries or damages caused to third parties. Comprehensive coverage includes damage to the insured vehicle.

Benefits: Covers repair costs for damages to the insured vehicle, liability for injuries or damages caused to others in accidents, and sometimes includes additional benefits like roadside assistance or legal expenses coverage.

Average cost: Car insurance premiums vary widely based on factors like vehicle type, age, driving experience, and coverage level. Annual premiums can range from SGD 500 to several thousand dollars.

Critical Illness Insurance

Coverage: Pays a lump sum upon diagnosis of critical illnesses such as cancer, heart attack, stroke, or major organ transplants.

Benefits: Provides financial support for medical treatments, recovery expenses, and lifestyle adjustments due to critical illnesses, supplementing income during treatment and recovery periods.

Average cost: Critical illness insurance premiums depend on coverage amount and age. Premiums for basic coverage can start from SGD 300 to SGD 1,000 per year, with higher coverage amounts or additional benefits increasing the cost.
